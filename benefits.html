Reduce Landfill Waste

Around 50 per cent of household waste is organic. As most greenhouse gas emissions from landfill come from decomposing organic matter, GREAT Sorts earth-cycle to easily reduce what goes in their general waste bin, tackle climate change and live more sustainably.

Enhance Soil Health

Macro-organisms consume millions of tonnes of organic wastes daily. Micro-organisms break it down and release nutrients back into the soil. This continues the biological cycle.

Promoting Biodiversity

Composting also plays a vital role in promoting biodiversity. It helps to create a healthy ecosystem by providing essential nutrients for microorganisms, insects, and other small creatures. This, in turn, attracts larger animals and birds, creating a balanced and diverse environment.
