Common Mistakes

Don't add meat or dairy, avoid plastic, and turn your compost regularly.

Best Practices

Mix greens and browns evenly, keep moisture balanced, and aerate weekly.

Materials to Avoid

Plastic, metal, glass, diseased plants, and pet waste.

Materials to Add

Fruit and vegetable scraps, coffee grounds, leaves, and small branches.

Moisture Control

Keep compost moist like a damp sponge, not soaked.

Aeration

Turn the pile regularly to add oxygen and speed up decomposition.
